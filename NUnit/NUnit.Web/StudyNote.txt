NUnit 
=====
Open Source Unit testing Framework. N stand for .NET

Why needed?
=========== 
to find defect ealier in the development lifecycle. 


Syntax 
======
Assert - tell the test runner whether a test has passed or failed
 --Assert.That(actual, expected)
 eg/ Assert.That(Calculator.Add(1,2), IsEqualTo(3));
